About Me

My name is Chad Doloan, and I'm a photographer specializing in environmental portraits. I love using the environment around me to capture people in interesting ways. On top of portraits, I have a great love for street and cityscape photography. Everyday is a new lesson learned and a chance for a new photo to be created. I will continue to do my best to capture and create great photos not just for me, but for everyone out there who supports and follows me. And hopefully I will help you guys continue to enjoy photography.
